We Make Music For Your 3D Platformer Experience

Music Haven Productions is a Video Game Music Production Company that Specializes in Platformer Video Games. A Platformer Game is a subgenre of Action Games where the main character navigates a series of levels to complete an assignment. There are also battles along the way where the player must defeat villains and bosses. We do anything from Singles to Soundtracks. Inspired by platformers of the early 2000s, when music was really intricate, we strive to keep the feeling alive with unique, addictive music that will keep you immersed in your gaming experience.
